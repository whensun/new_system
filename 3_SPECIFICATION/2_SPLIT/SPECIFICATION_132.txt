## 23.2. Half-Precision Computational Instructions

A new supported format is added to the format field of most instructions, as shown in Table 31.

Table 31. Format field encoding.

|   fmt field | Mnemonic   | Meaning                 |
|-------------|------------|-------------------------|
|          00 | S          | 32-bit single-precision |
|          01 | D          | 64-bit double-precision |
|          10 | H          | 16-bit half-precision   |
|          11 | Q          | 128-bit quad-precision  |

The half-precision  floating-point  computational  instructions  are  defined  analogously  to  their  singleprecision counterparts, but operate on half-precision operands and produce half-precision results.
