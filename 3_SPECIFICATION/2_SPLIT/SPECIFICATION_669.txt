## 34.5.1. ld

## Synopsis

Load doubleword to even/odd register pair, 32-bit encoding

## Mnemonic

ld rd, offset(rs1)

## Encoding (RV32)

<image>

## Description

Loads a 64-bit value into registers rd and rd+1 . The effective address is obtained by adding register rs1 to the sign-extended 12-bit offset.

Included in: Zilsd
