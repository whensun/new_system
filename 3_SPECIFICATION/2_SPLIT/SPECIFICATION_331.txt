## 30.4.2. Mapping for LMUL < 1

When  LMUL  <  1,  only  the  first  LMUL*VLEN/SEW  elements  in  the  vector  register  are  used.  The

remaining  space  in  the  vector  register  is  treated  as  part  of  the  tail,  and  hence  must  obey  the  vta setting.

```
Example, VLEN=128b, LMUL=1/4 Byte        F E D C B A 9 8 7 6 5 4 3 2 1 0 SEW=8b      - - - - - - - - - - - - 3 2 1 0 SEW=16b       -   -   -   -   -   -   1   0 SEW=32b           -       -       -       0
```
